A PROUD IRISH ROOTS STORY

Better with Dew Valley

Starting in Thurles, we are proud to be providing premium cooked bacon for over thirty years, supplying exceptional streaky bacon from our purpose-built facility where natural curing meets cutting-edge technique.

SMOKING TECHNOLOGY

Traditional smoke, modern control

Our smoking systems are designed to give you that familiar, authentic bacon character while tightly controlling consistency and quality. By carefully managing smoke time, wood selection and airflow, we can deliver profiles that range from subtle and sweet to bold and robust.

Working closely with customers, we can tune smoke levels to suit different applications – from retail packs and food-to-go sandwiches through to ready meals, pizzas and hot food counters.

  • Natural hardwood smoke options
  • Controlled, repeatable smoke intensity
  • Support for cleaner labels and product claims
  • Consistent colour and aroma across batches

Crafted kitchens, consistent quality.

Every Dew Valley product is prepared in state-of-the-art facilities, combining precise process control with the experience of dedicated production teams.
