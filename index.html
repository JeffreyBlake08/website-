SUSTAINABILITY COMMITMENT

Building a better tomorrow

At Dew Valley, we believe great food should be produced responsibly. From renewable energy and sustainable sourcing to waste reduction and community investment, we're committed to making choices that protect our planet and support the people who depend on it.

Our goal: Net zero carbon emissions by 2035 through renewable energy, sustainable sourcing, and innovative production methods

RENEWABLE ENERGY

Powering production sustainably

Our facility harnesses renewable energy sources including on-site solar installations and green energy partnerships. By investing in clean power, we're reducing our carbon footprint while maintaining the precise temperature and cooking controls our products demand.

We continuously monitor energy usage across all operations, implementing efficiency upgrades and exploring new technologies that allow us to produce more while consuming less.

  • Solar energy generation on-site
  • 100% renewable electricity sourcing
  • Energy-efficient production equipment
  • Real-time monitoring and optimization

ANIMAL WELFARE & RESPONSIBLE SOURCING

Ethical farming comes first

Every pig we source comes from farms where animal welfare is paramount. We work exclusively with suppliers who provide comfortable living conditions, proper nutrition, and humane treatment throughout the animals' lives. Our commitment to ethical farming isn't just about meeting standards—it's about exceeding them.

From the farms where our pigs are raised to our production facility, we maintain complete traceability and transparency. We believe that the quality of our bacon begins with the quality of life of the animals, and we're proud to partner with farmers who share our values.

  • Strict animal welfare standards across all farms
  • Full supply chain traceability from farm to fork
  • Support for local Irish farmers and communities
  • Regular audits and continuous improvement programs

Zero waste ambition

We've implemented comprehensive waste management systems that recover, reuse, and recycle materials throughout our operations. From repurposing production by-products to composting organic waste, we're constantly finding ways to close the loop.

Our packaging is designed for minimal environmental impact, using recyclable and compostable materials wherever possible. We work with partners who can process and reuse materials, ensuring that what leaves our facility has a second life.

Investing in our team and community

Sustainability extends beyond the environment—it's about creating a workplace where people thrive and supporting the communities where we operate. We provide ongoing training, safe working conditions, and opportunities for career development.

As a proud Irish company based in Thurles, we're committed to supporting local suppliers, creating employment, and contributing to the economic vitality of our region. Our success is built on the dedication of our team and the strength of our community partnerships.
